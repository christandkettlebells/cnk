Why I Do What I Do

Back in 2020, I became a wholesaler and CRM managing real estate portfolios for investors in one of the biggest and booming areas living in Houston, TX. I made a LOT of money, and had a stunning view over the city.

I had a nice apartment, a fancy red car, spent lunches and dinners with clients who talked in millions. Managing clients, leads, and selling a high-ticket membership. And I was good at my job, too.

Yet internally, I felt dead. My health was in shambles, my mental and emotional state in complete disarray, and I had no conscious relationship with God. I was Little Miss Perfect on the outside, and dying on the inside.

In 2022 I gave up the money, and completely changed industries — real estate investment to Personal Training.

Most trainers specialize in something. And over the years being a trainer, I noticed a weird trend. There are lots of online trainers. Most work with women. But then I started noticing — less trainers working with women working in the corporate/professional world. Even less who integrated Christian faith and fitness.

To this day, I have not seen a single online trainer specializing specifically in Christian women working in the corporate/professional world.

That's when Christ & Kettlebells was born. For women like you who feel like they've tried everything and are tired.

  • Tired of trying again just to fail.
  • Tired of trying to fit a meal plan or exercise program that the girl in the office swore by, and tired from the disappointment of it not working.
  • Tired of acting like you have it all together when you're falling apart on the inside.
  • Tired of hiding the guilt and shame of "I should get this by now".
  • Tired of the "I'm doing great" with a painted smile in the office halls. Day in, day out.
  • Tired of having a half in, half out relationship with God, and living with the incomprehensible demoralization that comes along with it.

This is why I built C&K. You do not have to trudge this road alone. There is a solution, specific to you and your life, that will get you results. So, let me help you lose the weight in your body and heart, build your relationship with God that will change your health and life in the most incredible way.
